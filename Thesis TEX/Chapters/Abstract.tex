\begin{abstract}
  I comparison of several metalearning strategies in order to ascertain the truth of the no free lunch
  theorem as applies to metalearning strategies is performed. The information needed in order to validate
  the performance of each metalearning strategy was gathered and stored in a database. Each metalearning
  strategy was then compared in terms of their accuracies, training times, and rate correct scores across
  the various metabases stored within the database.

\end{abstract}
